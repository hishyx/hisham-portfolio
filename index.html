About

Engineering philosophy and approach

I'm a backend-focused full-stack developer trained at Brocamp. My engineering approach centers on understanding problems deeply before writing code—I believe strong architectural decisions upstream prevent countless headaches downstream.

My philosophy: build → break → fix → improve. Rather than following tutorials, I learn by designing real systems, encountering failures, and iterating on solutions.

What excites me is the intersection of backend logic and user experience—designing intuitive APIs, databases that scale gracefully, and systems that don't require constant firefighting.
